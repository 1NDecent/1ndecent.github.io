Newcomer initial steps
  • Teleport your base near the majority of allies.
🛡 Daily contribution
  • Read the Alliance notice.
  • Always tap the 🤝 icon to help other members whenever it appears.
  • Enable rally auto-join once every 8 hours.
  • Initiate Boomer rallies 5 times.
  • Initiate or join Boomer rallies 30 times.
  • Gather resources from Alliance Wood/Food 1 time.
  • Fulfill Alliance tasks:
    • Gifts - Collect all chests.
    • Alliance Techs - Donate in full once every 4 hours.
    • Milestone - Achieve the target score of the active milestone.
  • Participate in Alliance-related events:
    • Zombie Siege - Simply keep all your troops at home and heal during this event.
    • The Tyrant - Coordinate with other members to rally against The Tyrant.
    • Territory Triumph - Coordinate with others and march your troops to attack a city.
📈 Daily self improvement
  • Collect resources within your own base. (duh!)
  • Claim all the free rewards:
    • VIP
    • Time-limited
    • Event Center
  • Participate in time-limited, special days, and daily events:
    • Champion Duel - Sign-up all your heroes.
    • Full Preparedness - Obtain the golden chest in every category.
    • Greatest Governor - Achieve the highest rank possible in active category.
    • Mines Melee - Gather highest-grade resources 4 times.
    • Zombie Hunter - Gather any world resources until you reach the full goal.
    • Defeat any Creeps until you reach the full goal.
  • Escort intercity trade trucks 4 times.
  • Participate in the Alliance chat.
